# Know your IDKs: Crafting the PERFECT Prompt

> **Track**: Principled AI Coding (Lesson 3)
> **Level**: Beginner

## Summary

Master the art of writing precise and effective prompts for coding tasks.

## Key Concepts

- **Keyword Usage**: Using the right phrases to communicate complex requirements.
- **IDK (I Don't Know)**: Identifying what the model doesn't know and supplying it.
- **Precision**: Moving from vague requests to specific instructions.
