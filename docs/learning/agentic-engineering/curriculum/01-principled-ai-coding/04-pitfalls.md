# How to Suck at AI Coding: Common Pitfalls

> **Track**: Principled AI Coding (Lesson 4)
> **Level**: Intermediate

## Summary

Learn from common mistakes and discover proven solutions to AI Coding challenges.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Overloaded Contexts**: Stuffing too much irrelevant info.
2. **Misguided Strategies**: Asking for too much at once.
3. **Cheap Models**: Using weak models for complex reasoning.

## Solutions

- Keep context lean.
- Break down tasks.
- Use SOTA models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet, GPT-4o) for coding.
